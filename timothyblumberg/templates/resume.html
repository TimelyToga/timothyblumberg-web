So many projects

The fact that the creative legwork must be put off to a later date so that things deemed more important may be worked on instead is rather preposterous to consider. However, it does seem to follow the classic, pragmatic order, that the act of creation be left to others deemed more capable. Absurd that we as a society have enabled the abstraction of our roles in society to expand to the point that we have creators of structure and creators of content.
