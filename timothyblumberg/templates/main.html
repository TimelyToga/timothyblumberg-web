LearnThings

An app that quietly teaches you.

July 2014

LearnThings allows you to study over an extended period of time. Instead of cramming for a large chunk of time before an exam, one could use LearnThings to space things out. LearnThings uses notifications to ask you questions, which you can directly interact with, or tap through to grade yourself. LearnThings is at most two taps per interaction, so the experience is left light. LearnThings knows when you need to have mastered your material, so it paces the testing accordingly so you are perfectly prepared. Link coming soon.
